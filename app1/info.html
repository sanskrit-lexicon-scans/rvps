Ṛgveda Prātiśākhya

app1 provides access to pages based on (patala, sūtra)
where patala is the chapter number, sūtra is the verse.
Example: https://sanskrit-lexicon-scans.github.io/rvps/app1/?16,53

app2 provides access to pages based on ipage (image page number).
Example: https://sanskrit-lexicon-scans.github.io/rvps/app2/?13
